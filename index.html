Mr Robin Williams

The man who made the world smile

Here's a brief montage of Robin McLaurin William's life.
  • 1951-Born in Chicago, Illnois
  • 1976-Began performing Stand up comedy in the San Francisco Bay Area.
  • 1970s to 1980s-Started reaching a wider audience, and won a Grammy Award for Best Comedy Album
  • Partly due the stress of stand-up, he started using drugs and alcohol.
  • 1978-Starred in the movie Off the Wall.
  • 1983-Played in the movie An Evening with Robin Williams.
  • 1986-Williams co-hosted the 58th Academy Awards
  • 2002-He headlined his own one-man show, Robin Williams: Live on Broadway, which played at the Broadway theatre
  • 2006-He starred in five movies including Man of the Year and The Night Listener.
  • Married five times, the latest one coming up in 2011, and had three children.
  • 2014-Died aged 63.
